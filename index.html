What is 2Day?

2Day is our project. It is a website where people can organize their daily activities in an easy and fun way. Specially it is oriented to people with psychological disabilities and their keepers. For this reason, this website is simple and intuitive. You only need drag any activity and drop it for adding it in your schedule.

Why do this project?

The objective of this project is make easier the life of people with psychological disabilities and their keepers. We think this can be a good tool for learning the sequence of daily routine. Stating routines is important because keep a predictable atmosphere that give security, trust and comfort.
